About Wonkydoodles

This is a clone of Google Creative's "Quick, Draw!". The purpose of this website is to showcase my abilities. This project can be divided into two parts - the training of the neural net and the website itself.

Contact me if you have any questions, other inquiries or even want to hire me!

The Neural Net

As opposed to the original "Quick, Draw!", which is a Recurrent Neural Net, the network used here is a Convolutional Neural Net. Their accuracies are similar at about 70% for their most probable guess. Take a look at the github repo!

This Website

There is nothing much to say about the website, since you have probably seen it for yourself if you are reading this. It is a Python-Flask-Server deployed on Microsoft Azure. Take a look at the github repo!
